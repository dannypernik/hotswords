Dear HOTSWORDS family,

First of all, I want to say thank you so much to all of you for the positivity and loyal support you’ve shown us over the past months. It has been a great motivation to me personally to hear how much you’ve enjoyed our food and service. It is with a heavy heart that we have decided to shift our restaurant to catering only. The reasons behind this decision are complicated, but I do hope and trust that HOTSWORDS will be able to fully open again one day in the future.

I also want to give a big thank you to my team, who have shown so much dedication to their work and without whom HOTSWORDS would never have been possible. I am very happy to share that we have all decided to stay together as we transition to the next phase.

Please do not hesitate to reach out to me through the contact form at the bottom of this page for catering requests or anything else. It is a pleasure and honor to serve you!

Blessings,
Mo Alkhdour, Owner and Head Chef
